About Us:

Canadian Proud strives to unite Canada by advertising Canadian businesses, creators, artists or any other Canadian with a dream. Show us what being Canadian truly means!

How It Works:

If you are a Canadian, you are welcome to buy a 20px by 20px square for basically the same price as a timmies coffee, $2. Other sizes include 50px x 50px for $12 or 100px x 100px for $50. For other formatting options please email us at ~ WeAreCanadianProud@gmail.com

Payment

Sorry for the inconvenience, but currently Canadian Proud is only accepting E-transfers or PayPal. We hope to grant other payment options in the future!

Process
  • 1: Find an image and link you believe is suitable to depict your brand (ensure you have all rights to the image and it does not violate copyright guidelines)
  • 2: Decide how many pixels you are willing to purchase and if your image will properly be formatted accordingly
  • 3: Email us at ~ WeAreCanadianProud@gmail.com please include the following:
  • Your name
  • City and postal code of your location
  • The image that will be uploaded
  • The number of pixels that will be purchased (20px x 40px = 16$)
  • A link that the user will be taken to if your image is clicked
  • 4: Wait for our reply, confirmation and bill
  • 5: Process payment
  • 6: After 1-5 business days your image and link will be uploaded to Canadian Proud!
