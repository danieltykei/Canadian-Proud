About Us:

Canadian Proud desires to unite Canada by advertising Canadian businesses, creators, artists or any other Canadian with a dream. By doing so we hope we can show the world what Canada is truly made of

How It Works:

If you are a Canadian, you are welcome to buy a 10px by 10px square for the same price as a timmies coffee, 2$. There if no maximum number of squared pixels one is allowed to purchase. We do ask to me mindful about the space and give smaller organizations a fighting chance

Payment

Sorry for the inconvenience, but current Canadian proud is only accepting e-transfers or paypal. We hope to grant other payment option in the future

Process
  • 1: Find an image and link you think is suitable to depict your brand (we ask that the images are your images and do not violate copyright guidelines)
  • 2: Decide how many pixel you are willing to buy and if you image will properly be formatted accordingly
  • 3: Email us at ~ WeAreCanadianProud@gmail.com please include the following:
  • Your name
  • City and postal code of where you are located
  • The image that will be uploaded
  • The number of pixels that will be purchased
  • A link that the user will be taken too if your image is clicked
  • 4: Wait for our reply, confirmation and bill
  • 5: Process payment
  • 6: After 3-5 business days your image and link will be uploaded to Canadian Proud!
